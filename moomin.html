Moomin Madness

About the Game

Moomin Madness is created in Monogames and is my entry for this years (2017) arcade machine competition at Malmö University. The Muskrat has predicted the worlds end and the Moomin Valley is in panic. In distress, Snorkmaiden entered the realm of coitus with Snufkin which caused Moomin to enrage. One thing led to another and now none will survive...

This game was solo project of mine. I coded everything and drew all the characters and UI components apart from the background images. The music is created by a friend of mine, Dj Broccoli, who can be found on soundcloud and spotify.
