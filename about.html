About Me

My name is Oliver Nyholm, and I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with specialization in Game Development from Malmö University.

My area of expertise is programming. I have created games with the legacy system XNA, now Monogames, coding in C#. My Bachelor's thesis, Madame Legume, was coded in Unity3D and I have also created games in Unreal Engine.

I have great interest of AI technologies, which I unfortunately have not programmed a lot of. The AI I have coded is simple AI and scripted boss fights for Johnny Punchfucker, a simple AI for the horror game Imminent using Unreal Engines behaviour Tree, A* pathfinding for ghosts in pacman and an evolutinoary algorithm for level generation in my bachelor's thesis.

I like to explore new challenges when working, and I consider myself being a fast learner. Especially when surrounded by experienced colleagues.
