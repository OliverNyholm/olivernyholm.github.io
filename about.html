About Me

My name is Oliver Nyholm, I am currently a programmer student at The Game Assembly. Before starting at TGA, I acquired a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with specialization in Game Development from Malmö University.

I'm a guy from the Aland Islands, Finland that somehow ended up in Malmö, and I've enjoyed living here for the past 5 years even though they have a serious shortage of good saunas. In my spare time I enjoy bouldering and playing games.

Most of my games are created with C++, in either TGA2Ds home engine or my school group, Wizard Loafs, own engine Yellowsnow. I have also created games with the legacy system XNA, now Monogames, coding in C#. My Bachelor's thesis, Madame Légume, was coded in Unity and I have also created games in Unreal Engine and Love2D, a Lua 2D Engine.

I like to explore new challenges when working, and I consider myself being a fast learner. At the Game Assembly, I often took a more responsible role in the group projects, where I would assigning meetings when needed, bring up concerns and I also did a lot of support programming. Whenever something in the engine was "burning" or someone from the other disciplines could not work due to this or that, I usually stopped my work and fixed their issues first, so no one would be hindered in their work.

Game Interest

My preference of games is mainly online multiplayer games. I have spent thousands of hours playing World of Warcraft, League of Legends and Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. As I dislike being bad at computer games, I end up grinding the games I play, reaching a high level in most games. Nowadays, I mostly play CSGO if I have time. Link to my Steam profile.

I also enjoy playing single player games. The genre of single player games that I enjoy are puzzle games such as Limbo, Inside, Portal 2 and Little Nightmares.
